SwipeGuide

Ready to start? →

SwipeGuide is a tool that helps you create step-by-step instructions from your photos and videos, and share them instantly on any device.

SwipeGuide is a StarterSquad project. Our founders got the idea when they were flying back to Amsterdam from StartupWeekend Timisoara. The development of this project started in the Munich airport. After the first bits of code were layered in, our community started getting involved to polish the design and set up an initial landing page. This has helped us asses interest in the tool and reach out to potential users.

Results

Ready to start? →

The project received a funding injection in mid 2014 and is currently being developed. SwipeGuide is scheduled to launch in the Fall of 2014.
