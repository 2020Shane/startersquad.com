Technical Feasibility Scan

A solid technical foundation is crucial for the success of Internet Startups, particularly in product marketing. To mitigate technical implementation risk in product development StarterSquad performs TFS. We will:

  • draw up a potential architecture;
  • work with the client to define a MVP;
  • estimate the amount of work;
  • identify technical risks such as: scalability, complexity or need for rare skills;
  • make a draft design that you can use in early marketing and pitch decks.

When investing in a new idea, there are business risks and technical risks. Financial investors ask would-be founders to scrutinize the business risks, but most technical investors are less experienced and fooled by the founders' enthusiasm. An investor should not rely on the founders’ technical team to identify risks early.

Business Viability Scan

StarterSquad developed a methodology to check the viability of your startup or innovation idea. Our evidence-based approach checks if your startup has the marks found in the most successful Startups:

  • Idea: the idea is superb and will make headlines.
  • Product/Market fit: your product will impress customers and there is a great market for your idea.
  • Team and Execution: your team and you have the power to realize your dream both technically and commercially.
  • Traction: the proof that your value proposition is appealing to customers.
  • Social Proof: the proof that investors are interested and comparable startups are in business.

The BVS consists of a questionnaire, research and workshops with your Startup team. You'll get a report that helps growing your business and acquiring investment. This scan is often combined with the TFS in technology-based Startups. These scans Lean Startup your Business.

Investor Readiness Check

Are you ready to convince investors to fund your Startup? The Startersquad IRC finds flaws and boosts the USP's of your Startup idea. After a TFS and BVS we can do an IRC covering:

  • Startup Idea Strengths: finds strengths and weaknesses in your Startup idea from an investor’s perspective. You'll have some homework for the second day.

  • Financing Options: points you in the direction where investment for your Startup idea can be found.
  • Pitch Deck & Skills: finally we will spend some time on pitch (deck) and your pitching skills.
