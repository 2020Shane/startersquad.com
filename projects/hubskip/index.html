Hubskip

Hubskip helps you find the destinations that match your interest while getting you the best price for your flight.

Project description

Hubskip sells city trips to all major destinations in world. They focus on a prediction system for flight prices. StarterSquad developed the online shop, including search, payment and integration with several b2b providers. The REST backend was done in Scala with Spray. The frontend was done with AngularJS.

Within 8 weeks we have built the full solution, right on time to demo at the Launch Festival 2013
