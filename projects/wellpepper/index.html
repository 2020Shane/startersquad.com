Early feedback to patients

Rehabilitation after an injury should be closely monitored by a medical specialist, for example a physiotherapist. Typically this happens with weekly sessions, but after a week of performing the exercise in the wrong way a lot of damage could be done.

This can be solved by regularly giving feedback on how the patient performs her exercises, but this is costly in the current setup of a physio therapist.

Asynchronous video chat

To solve this problem, Wellpepper allows physio therapists to chat with patients, using video messages and pictures they take on their mobile device. This allows patients to record their questions directly when doing the exercise, and it allows the physio therapist to respond during his day whenever he finds some time.

With the chat application developed by StarterSquad, Wellpepper is running pilots at several US hospitals. Feedback is very positive.
